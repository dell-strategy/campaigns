Recommended Strategic Topics (General IT)

Broad technology themes synthesized from the attendee profiles.

1. Modernizing Citizen Services & Digital Equity

Context for this room: Constituents expect consumer-grade digital experiences from their local government. However, legacy systems and technical debt are holding back these innovations.

Dell Value Prop to mention: How Dell's edge computing and hybrid cloud infrastructure allow cities to deploy "smart" services faster—from intelligent traffic management to robust online portals—bridging the digital divide efficiently.

Target Audience:

2. AI Readiness & Data Management Strategy

Context for this room: Public sector leaders want to implement GenAI, but their data is siloed across dozens of different municipal departments and state agencies.

Dell Value Prop to mention: Emphasize that "there is no AI without IA (Information Architecture)." Discuss Dell's unstructured data storage (PowerScale) and how we help SLG entities consolidate, secure, and prepare their data estates for private, secure AI initiatives.

Target Audience:

3. High Availability & Infrastructure Resilience

Context for this room: Downtime is not just an inconvenience in government; it impacts public safety, 911 dispatch, and critical utilities. 24/7 availability is non-negotiable.

Dell Value Prop to mention: Dell's end-to-end infrastructure solutions that guarantee uptime. From fault-tolerant servers running critical databases to Cyber Recovery vaults that ensure operations can be restored rapidly after any disruption (natural or cyber).

Target Audience:

4. Overcoming Budget Constraints with APEX

Context for this room: Funding cycles are rigid. SLG entities often struggle with massive upfront capital expenditures (CapEx) for IT overhauls, even when the technology is desperately needed today.

Dell Value Prop to mention: Shifting to an OpEx (Operational Expenditure) model through Dell APEX. Explain how consuming infrastructure "As-a-Service" allows cities and states to scale capacity up or down dynamically, paying only for what they use without massive upfront costs.

Target Audience:
