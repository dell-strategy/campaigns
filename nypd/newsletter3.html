Moving from reactive remediation to engineered resilience. How NYPD and Dell Technologies are consolidating footprint, executing historic infrastructure refreshes, and securing mission-critical applications.

The Executive Mandate

Technology availability at NYPD is inseparable from public safety. Prior to this transformation, increasing operational risks were driven by aging, unsupported infrastructure. Incremental fixes were no longer sufficient.

NYPD engaged Dell Technologies as a strategic partner to fully rearchitect the technology foundation. The objective: ensure continuous availability for systems today, while preparing for the operational demands of tomorrow.

"NYPD must operate in a Never Down technology environment."

— Core Modernization Directive

This transformation guarantees critical systems can operate with the speed and resilience required to support mission critical operations and AI driven capabilities [cite: 23].
