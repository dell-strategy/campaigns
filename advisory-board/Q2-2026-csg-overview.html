Recommended Strategic Topics (PC Dev)

Key endpoint hardware themes synthesized from the attendee profiles.

1. AI PCs & The Future of Public Sector Work

Context for this room: States and cities want the productivity benefits of GenAI (Copilot) for their workforce but are terrified of data leaving the device. They need local processing power.

Dell Value Prop to mention: Our new lineup of AI PCs with built-in NPUs allows SLG workers to run LLMs and AI tasks locally on the hardware, ensuring citizen data never goes to the public cloud while drastically improving battery life during video conferencing.

Target Audience:

2. Rugged Solutions for Extreme Environments

Context for this room: Law enforcement, utility workers, and facility managers require devices that can survive extreme heat, drops, and vehicle mounting. Standard laptops fail in these environments.

Dell Value Prop to mention: The evolution of the Latitude Rugged portfolio. Emphasize sunlight-readable screens, extreme temperature tolerance (especially for Phoenix and Texas attendees), and seamless transition from squad car docks to field operations.

Target Audience:

3. Hardware-Level Security & Compliance

Context for this room: Software security isn't enough anymore. Attendees are concerned about supply chain attacks and firmware vulnerabilities, particularly to meet CJIS (law enforcement) and state regulations.

Dell Value Prop to mention: How Dell builds "the industry's most secure commercial PCs." Discuss Hardware Root of Trust, Dell SafeBIOS, and physical privacy features (camera shutters, electronic privacy screens) that come standard to support zero-trust architectures.

Target Audience:

4. Endpoint Lifecycle & Sustainability

Context for this room: Managing the deployment, maintenance, and end-of-life of thousands of devices is a massive drain on municipal IT budgets. Furthermore, sustainability requirements are increasingly written into government RFPs.

Dell Value Prop to mention: PC-as-a-Service (PCaaS) for predictable budgeting, factory provisioning so devices ship ready-to-work directly to remote employees, and our industry-leading use of recycled/ocean-bound plastics in OptiPlex and Latitude designs.

Target Audience:
