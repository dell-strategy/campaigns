What is a "Program"?

In the context of Dell SLG Strategy, a Program (or Sales Play) is not just a product launch. It is a coordinated, cross-functional initiative designed to capture a specific, time-bound market opportunity.

✓
End-to-End Alignment

Connects Strategy, Product, Marketing, and Sales Execution.

✓
Solution Bundling

Hardware + Software + Partners + Funding Mechanisms.

✓
Targeted Narrative

Addresses a specific vertical problem (e.g., Rural Health) rather than a spec sheet.
