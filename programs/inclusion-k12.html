Dell as a Strategic K–12 Partner

Dell Technologies is more than a Chromebook provider. We are a strategic education partner helping districts design inclusive, future-ready learning ecosystems. In addition to delivering durable Chromebooks at scale, Dell invests in student programs, experiential learning, and funding support that:

  • Expand access to STEM, IT, and digital skills for all students
  • Strengthen school & district tech ecosystems with student-powered support
  • Unlock new funding streams to sustain innovation over time

These value-add programs are designed to complement [District Name]’s Chromebook initiative and directly support student achievement, equity, and workforce readiness.

Girls Who Game

Developing STEM & leadership skills through game-based learning

An extracurricular program created by Dell Technologies with Microsoft and Intel. Open to all, it uses gaming—especially Minecraft-based challenges—to help students, particularly girls and underrepresented learners, build STEM and future-ready skills in a supportive community.

Key Student Outcomes

  • Build computational & critical thinking skills via real-world Minecraft challenges
  • Strengthen communication, collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving
  • Grow self-efficacy and confidence in STEM & technology pathways
  • Engage in projects aligned to UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Student TechCrew

Student-led IT support that builds real-world skills

An educational program from Dell Technologies that partners with schools to create student-run technology help desks. Students gain hands-on IT experience while providing real support for their school community.

Outcomes

  • Foundational IT & troubleshooting skills
  • Earn industry certifications (Dell TechDirect)
  • Customer service & communication skills
  • Authentic career & college readiness

School Benefits

  • Student-led repair & update help desks
  • Additional on-campus tech maintenance capacity
  • Diverse pipeline of student tech leaders

Alienware Mobile Gaming Lounge

High-impact esports & STEM engagement

For very large K–12 initiatives, Dell Technologies can augment Girls Who Game and esports programming with a ~650 sq ft mobile esports environment designed to inspire students, showcase career pathways, and celebrate district innovation.

Highlights

  • Mobile or semi-permanent structure with desks, server, networking, and a 55" Dell display
  • Seats 12 students simultaneously with Alienware gaming laptops, displays, peripherals, and headsets
  • Exclusive Alienware branding (District manages ongoing maintenance, transport & brand adherence)

Serves as a centerpiece for STEM events, Girls Who Game showcases, esports tournaments, and career-connected learning days.

Dell Grants Support Program

Funding the future of learning

To help districts overcome budget limitations, Dell offers a complimentary service for public sector customers that connects K–12 systems with targeted grant funding to accelerate technology and digital inclusion initiatives.

At no cost, customers receive:

  • Real-time K-12 funding insights
  • Customized research reports
  • Strategic Consultant sessions
  • Proposal Review & Editing

Proven Impact

Over $300M in tracked grants enabled for public sector customers. Funding includes wins for rugged devices, device refresh, digital/GenAI innovation, storage modernization, cybersecurity, and disaster recovery.
